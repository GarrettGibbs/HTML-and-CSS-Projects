    Why Penguin?:
    Penguins serve not only as our mascot, but also as our inspiration.
    They are stoic and endlessly hardworking, putting everything they have into the teamwork and community they are a part of.
    At the same time, they are absurd and ridiculous, waddling with the best of them.
    We strive to embody both sides of the penguin, hardworking and fun-loving! About the Company:
    Penguin Productions is a theatre company located just outside of Newberg, Oregon.
    Our mission is to entertain and inspire our community by creating theater for a contemporary world.
    To that end, we are dedicated to creating a diverse ensemble of artists and technicians for each season.
    Currently operating on a outdoor stage nestled in a field of hazelnut trees, Penguin Productions offers professional-quality shows in the heart of scenic wine country.
